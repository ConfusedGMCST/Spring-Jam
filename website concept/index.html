About the Game

Teamwork Makes the Dream Work

Earth Guy and Air Girl is a cooperative 2D puzzle-platformer where two elemental heroes must combine their unique powers to conquer increasingly tricky levels. Earth Guy's brute strength and underwater ability pair perfectly with Air Girl's agility and vent-gliding finesse.


Work together — or control both heroes solo — to push buttons, cross chasms, dodge hazards, and unlock the ancient elemental gates. Every puzzle is a dance between earth and air.

🌿
Earth pushes heavy blocks & presses switches
💨
Air glides through vents & reaches high platforms
🌊
Earth can dive underwater; Air cannot
✨
Fuse together for ultimate elemental power

Meet the Heroes

Two Elements

Earth Guy

  • 💪
    Heavy and powerful — can push boulders
  • 🌊
    Can dive and breathe underwater
  • 🔘
    Activates ground-level pressure buttons
  • 📦
    Moves heavy objects and blocks
⚠️ Low jump height — can't reach high ledges

Air Girl

  • 🌬️
    Floats and glides through the air
  • 💨
    Passes through wind vents & air tunnels
  • 🦅
    Super high jump — reaches any platform
  • ⚡
    Fast, agile movement and wall-cling
⚠️ Cannot touch water · Cannot press heavy buttons
Key Features

What Makes It Special

⚗️

Fusion Mechanic

Combine Earth Guy and Air Girl into a single unstoppable hero with all abilities. Unlock hidden areas and secret paths only reachable in fusion form.

🧩

Puzzle-First Design

Every level is a handcrafted brain-teaser. No filler — each stage introduces a fresh mechanic or twist on a familiar one.

👥

Co-op Play

Play with a friend locally! The puzzles are designed to be fun and fair.

📈

Rising Difficulty

Start gentle, end legendary. Five worlds of escalating challenge, from breezy meadows to volcanic depths and storm-swept skies.
